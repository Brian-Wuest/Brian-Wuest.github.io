Mod Info

For Players:

This mod is all about providing items that lets you generate buildings quickly and safely. Once you get one of these items you don't have to worry about a pesky zombie getting in the way when are trying to design your dream home.

The slideshow shows some of the buildings which you can generate with this mod.

Information on all of the generatable buildings and other bits and bobbles available in this mod can be found here.

You find all of the available downloads for this mod here.

Happy building!
